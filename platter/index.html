Got Questions?

Platter is a membership program by Bitroot that gives founders and teams access to exclusive deals, premium tool credits, a founder network, and priority support — all designed to help you build and scale faster while spending less.

Platter is open to anyone building a product or company — from solo indie hackers to small teams and early-stage startups. You don't need to be a current Bitroot client to join.

Our partner network includes cloud hosting providers, design tools, analytics platforms, communication software, and more. Credits and discounts range from extended free trials to up to 90% off annual subscriptions. The specific offers rotate and expand as we onboard new partners.

Platter operates on a one-time annual payment — there are no recurring charges or auto-renewals. Because benefits are accessible immediately upon payment, we don't offer refunds or mid-term cancellations. Your access remains active through the end of your billing period, and you won't be charged again unless you choose to renew.

Unlike generic perk programs, Platter is curated by Bitroot's team — people who actively build technology for startups. We only include tools and partners we've personally vetted and would use ourselves. Plus, you get direct access to our team for guidance, not just a list of coupon codes.
