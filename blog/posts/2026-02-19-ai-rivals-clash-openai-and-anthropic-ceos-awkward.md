## Introduction to the Rivalry
We just spotted an exciting update from the India AI Impact Summit that's worth sharing with the community. The CEOs of OpenAI and Anthropic, Sam Altman and Dario Amodei, had a notable moment on stage where they chose not to hold hands during a group photo.

## The Awkward Moment
Here's what caught our attention: the two CEOs, who were standing next to each other, raised their fists instead of holding hands. This awkward encounter reveals the underlying tension between the two companies as they compete for dominance in the AI market.
