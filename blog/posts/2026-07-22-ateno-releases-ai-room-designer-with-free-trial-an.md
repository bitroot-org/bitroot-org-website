Ateno just launched an AI‑powered room redesign service that turns a single photo into realistic redesigns and an interactive 3‑D model, with a free tier available immediately. The Pro subscription unlocks the “Reimagine” feature and full‑resolution 3D exploration.

## How it works
Upload a JPEG of any room, and the backend model generates multiple layout suggestions that blend the original dimensions with AI‑chosen furniture. Each suggestion can be inspected in a WebGL 3‑D viewer that lets you rotate, zoom, and click on items to see a shoppable link.

## Pricing and feature tiers
The free tier provides up to three redesigns per month and a limited 3‑D preview that caps at low‑resolution textures. The paid Pro tier, priced at $9.99 / month, adds unlimited redesigns, the “Reimagine” button that swaps entire style themes, and a full‑quality 3‑D experience. All furniture links point to retailers that have partnered with [Ateno](https://ateno.co/software), so the cost of the items themselves is separate.

## Caveats to keep in mind
AI suggestions can include items that are out of stock or mismatched to regional availability, leading to false‑positive “shop now” links. The 3‑D viewer runs entirely in the browser, so performance may degrade on older laptops. Because the service is hosted, you are dependent on Ateno’s uptime and data‑privacy policies.

## When to try it
If you need quick visual mockups for a pitch deck or want to test a new product line without hiring a designer, spin up a free account and run a single room through the pipeline. Monitor the conversion rate of the shoppable links against your own catalog before deciding whether the $9.99 Pro tier is justified.
