## Introduction to Parallel Agents
We just spotted an exciting update from Zed that's worth sharing with the community, and it's all about Parallel Agents. This feature allows developers to mix and match any agent, run them all at once, and manage everything from a new Threads Sidebar. 
 

## What Caught Our Attention
Here's what caught our attention about Parallel Agents: the ability to use multiple agents across all projects from one window. The implications are huge, as it enables seamless integration and management of various tools and workflows. 
 

## Key Features and Benefits
Some of the key features and benefits of Parallel Agents include:
* Mix and match any agent, including Claude Agent, Codex, Zed's agent, or anything on ACP
* Run multiple agents simultaneously, streamlining workflows and boosting productivity
* Manage everything from a new Threads Sidebar, providing a unified view of all projects

Here's why this matters for developers: Parallel Agents has the potential to revolutionize the way we work on projects. By allowing multiple agents to run at the same time, developers can automate tasks, collaborate more effectively, and focus on high-priority work.
