## The Problem with Font Pairing
What caught our attention about Typevibe is its ability to simplify this process. Font pairing is hard, and it's one of those problems that sounds simple until you're deep into Google Fonts with multiple tabs open and still stuck with the classic 'inter'. 

## How Typevibe Works
Typevibe gives you a head start by recommending unique font pairings along with 32 design templates that instantly show you how those fonts actually look. The features of Typevibe include:
* Unique font pairing recommendations
* 32 design templates for various use cases, such as editorials, posters, menu cards, and data dashboards
* Live updates as you explore different pairings
