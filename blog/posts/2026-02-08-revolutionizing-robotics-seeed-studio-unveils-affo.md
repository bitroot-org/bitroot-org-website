## Introduction to reBot Arm B601
We just spotted an exciting update from Seeed Studio that's worth sharing with the community - the release of their own affordable and open-source robotic arm, the reBot Arm B601. This innovative design targets a sub-$1,000 build budget, making robotics more accessible to developers and hobbyists. The reBot Arm B601 boasts impressive performance specs, including a maximum reach of 650mm and a payload of at least 1.5kg.

## What Sets reBot Arm B601 Apart
Here's what caught our attention about this release - Seeed Studio's commitment to documentation and software support. The company is rolling out a comprehensive package, including a complete Bill of Materials, assembly instructions, 3D models, and free learning courses. Some of the key features and benefits include:
* Complete open-source design for a six-DoF + gripper robotic arm
* Surprisingly respectable performance specs
* Commitment to documentation and software support
* Support for ROS2, LeRobot, Pinocchio, Isaac Sim, and more with a comprehensive Python SDK
