We just spotted an exciting update from Y Combinator that's worth sharing with the community - their renowned Startup School is making its way to India. This is huge news for the local startup ecosystem, as it brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to aspiring entrepreneurs. Here's why this matters for developers and founders in India.

## What to Expect from Startup School
The event will feature talks from successful founders like Harshil Mathur of Razorpay, Vidita Athrey of Meesho, and more. Some of the key highlights include:
* Insights from experienced entrepreneurs who have built and scaled successful startups
* Sessions with YC partners, offering valuable guidance and feedback
* Networking opportunities with the best builders and hackers from across the country
