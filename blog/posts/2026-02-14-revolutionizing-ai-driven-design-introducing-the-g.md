## What is Design Canvas?
Here's what caught our attention about this update: Design Canvas is a drag & drop editor that enables developers to work seamlessly with AI agents like Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex. With this tool, developers can now build and iterate on their UI designs without the need for endless prompting, making the design process faster and more efficient.

## Key Features and Benefits
Some of the key features and benefits of Design Canvas include:
* A user-friendly drag & drop interface that makes it easy to build and iterate on UI designs
* Seamless integration with popular AI agents like Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex
