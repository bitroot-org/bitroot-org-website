## Introduction to Interactive Transcripts
We just spotted an exciting update from kepano that's worth sharing with the community: Obsidian Reader now makes video transcripts interactive. This means you can scrub, highlight, and auto-scroll through transcripts, making video consumption more engaging and efficient.

## What Caught Our Attention
Here's what caught our attention about this update: the potential to revolutionize the way we watch and interact with videos. With interactive transcripts, you can easily navigate through videos, highlight important points, and even auto-scroll through the transcript.

## Key Features and Benefits
Some of the key features and benefits of interactive transcripts include:
* Scrubbing: easily navigate through the video by dragging your cursor through the transcript
* Highlighting: highlight important points in the transcript for easy reference
* Auto-scrolling: automatically scroll through the transcript as the video plays
* Improved engagement: interactive transcripts make video watching more engaging and interactive
