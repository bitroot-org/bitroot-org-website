## Introduction to Happycapy
We just spotted an exciting update from Jarod Xu that's worth sharing with the community - Happycapy is now open to everyone. This means that the agent-native computer, which was previously limited, can now be accessed by anyone, anywhere, through their browser or phone.

## Key Features and Benefits
Here's what caught our attention about Happycapy:
* Powered by Claude Code + MiniMax, including Opus 4.6 and Minimax M2.5
* Your own secure cloud sandbox for running agent teams and automating tasks
* No installs required - just run and start exploring the possibilities

## Implications and Potential Use Cases
What's really exciting about Happycapy is its potential to democratize access to agent-native computing. This could have significant implications for developers and tech enthusiasts, enabling them to build and automate complex tasks without the need for extensive infrastructure or setup. The community is buzzing about the potential use cases, from automating repetitive tasks to building custom AI-powered agents.

## Why This Matters
Here's why this matters for developers: Happycapy offers a unique all-in-one solution that eliminates the need for multiple tools and installations. With its secure cloud sandbox, users can focus on building and testing their agent teams without worrying about the underlying infrastructure. This could lead to faster development cycles, increased productivity, and more innovative solutions.
