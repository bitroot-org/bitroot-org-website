## Introduction to Agentic Payments
We just spotted an exciting update from Razorpay and NPCI that's worth sharing with the community - the introduction of Agentic Payments on Claude. This platform makes it possible to shop with AI, allowing people to order food, groceries, and everyday essentials without ever leaving the conversation.

## What Makes Agentic Payments Possible
What caught our attention about this update is the potential to complete transactions on a user's behalf, ensuring a seamless shopping journey. The key to this is Agentic Payments, a new payment capability built by Razorpay in partnership with NPCI. Some of the features of Agentic Payments include:
* Secure payments directly into the agentic experience
* Ability to complete transactions on a user's behalf
* Seamless and transparent payment process

## Why This Matters for Developers
Here's why this matters for developers - Agentic Payments has the potential to revolutionize the way we think about AI-powered commerce. By enabling AI to securely complete transactions, we can create more intuitive and user-friendly shopping experiences. The implications of this are vast, with potential use cases including:
* Voice-powered commerce
* AI-powered personal shopping assistants
* Seamless transactions across multiple platforms

## The Future of AI-Powered Commerce
The community is buzzing about the potential of Agentic Payments to transform the way we shop and pay online. With India already one of the largest AI-native markets in the world, this innovation is set to have a significant impact. As Rashmee Lahon notes, 'Agentic commerce doesn't need to be forced into Indian behavior. It fits naturally into how Indians already live, shop, and pay.'

In conclusion, the introduction of Agentic Payments on Claude is an exciting development that has the potential to transform the way we think about AI-powered commerce. With its seamless and secure payment process, Agentic Payments is set to revolutionize the way we shop and pay online.
