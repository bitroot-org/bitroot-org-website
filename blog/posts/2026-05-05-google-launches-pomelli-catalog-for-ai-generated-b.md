Google today launched Pomelli Catalog, a free web tool that lets you add product or service listings and instantly generates personalized marketing campaigns and high‑quality photoshoots tailored to your brand. The service is reachable at [Pomelli Catalog](http://labs.google.com/pomelli) and was announced in an [announcement tweet](https://x.com/i/status/2051693177053340073).

## How it works
You upload a CSV or fill a simple form with product names, descriptions, and optional images. Pomelli’s backend runs a generative model that assembles copy, targeting parameters, and AI‑rendered visuals, then returns a shareable campaign bundle.

## What you get for free
The free tier produces unlimited campaign drafts, each with a headline, body copy, and up to five AI‑generated photos. There is no watermark and the assets can be downloaded as PNG/JPEG files.

## Caveats to consider
Because the images are synthetically created, they may lack the nuance of a professional shoot and could trigger brand‑consistency reviews. The model also relies on the quality of the input data; sparse or ambiguous product descriptions often lead to generic copy. At present there is no API, so automation must be manual, and the service is only hosted on Google’s labs domain, which could be discontinued without notice.

## When to try it
If you need rapid mock‑ups for internal pitch decks or want to experiment with AI‑augmented branding without spending on a design agency, spin up Pomelli Catalog now and test a few products. Keep an eye on the lab site for any future pricing or feature changes.
