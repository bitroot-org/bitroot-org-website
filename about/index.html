About Us

BITROOT POWERHOUSE PVT LTD

Bitroot Powerhouse Pvt Ltd is a technology and marketing company registered in India. Founded in 2023, we help startups and companies build their technology foundation from the ground up.

Our mission is simple: help founders and companies go from zero to one by being the technology backbone for their business.

WHAT WE DO

We offer a range of services designed to support businesses at every stage of their growth:

  • Product Development - End-to-end product design and engineering for web and mobile applications.
  • Website Design & Development - Custom websites built to convert visitors into customers.
  • App Design & Development - Native and cross-platform mobile applications.
  • eCommerce Development - Online stores and digital commerce solutions.
  • Digital Marketing - Growth strategies, SEO, and performance marketing.

OUR PRODUCTS

Platter (available at platter.bitroot.org) is our subscription-based platform offering exclusive rewards, partner deals, and premium tools for founders. With 588+ partner perks across design tools, cloud hosting, analytics, and communication software, Platter helps early-stage startups save significantly on the tools they need to build and scale.
